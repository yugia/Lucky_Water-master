Location: Chilliwack, Canada

Lucky Water comes from the underground river of the melting water of the ancient Lucky Glaciers which is part of Canada's famous Rocky Mountains. The location of the underground river and our manufacturing facility is in Chilliwack, British Columbia, Canada, awarded many times as the Canadian city with "Canada's Best Drinking Water". Lucky Glaciers is in the high altitude of the latitude region of 36°- 46°. This area is covered by untouched snow and ice, away from human pollution and has an annual PM of 2.5 with an average of 4.9 micrograms per cubic meter. In other words, an environment as pure as it can be.
